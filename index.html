Real Madrid Football Club

About Us

Welcome to the heart of Real Madrid Football Club, where excellence thrives and legends are made. Established in 1902, our club stands as a beacon of football greatness, with a legacy steeped in triumph and tradition.
Players like Cristiano Ronaldo, Karim Benzema, Sergio Ramos, and Gareth Bale, among other renowned names, have also proudly worn the white jersey of Real Madrid Football Club.

Legacy

Real Madrid isn't just a football club, it is a dynasty of champions. With a record number of domestic league titles and a storied legacy in the UEFA Champions League, our team continues to push the boundaries of greatness.

Stadium

The Santiago Bernabeu Stadium is the home of Real Madrid Football Club. It is one of the most iconic stadiums in the world and has been the site of countless historic moments in football history.

Trophies

  • 14 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 35 La Liga titles
  • 12 Copa del Rey titles
